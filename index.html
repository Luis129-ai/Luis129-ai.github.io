Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Thank you for choosing to play Unlimited Sudoku. As a solo developer, I respect your privacy and am comitted to being transparent about how this app operates.

Summary

My philosophy is simple: I do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal data. The app is designed to function entirely on your device, with minimal external data usage solely for the purpose of enabling iCloud sync and processing payments through Apple.

Information Collection and Use

I want you to understand exactly what data is used within the app:

1. iCloud Data

To provide a seamless experience across your devices, Unlimited Sudoku uses Apple's built-in iCloud service NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore to sync your game state between your iPhone devices signed into the same Apple ID.

  • Who handles this data? This data is transmitted, stored, and processed entirely by Apple. I do not operate any servers and cannot access, read, or retrieve this data.

  • What is stored? Only non-personal, application specific data. This data is coins collected, reveals count, lives count, experience points, avatar, and streak.

2. In-App Purchases (Subscriptions)

Unlimited Sudoku offers renewable subscriptions (1 month, 3 months, and 1 year) that removes gameplay limitations.

  • What I receive: When you purchase a subscription, Apple's notifies Unlimited Sudoku about the subscription and unlocks the features for you.

  • Suscription Data: To manage or cancel your subscription, you must do so through your Apple ID account settings on your device or going into Settings Tab on Unlimited Sudoku and tapping on Manage Subscription.

3. No Data Collection

I do not use any third-part analytics tools, advertising frameworks, or tracking software. I do not ask for your name, email address, or location.

Data Sharing with Third Parties

I do not sell, trade or transfer any information to outside parties. The only transfer that occurs is directly between your device and Apple's iCloud servers.

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages. I do not collect any personal information from users, including children under the age of 13. Because the app has no data collection mechanisms, all users are subject to the same high standard of privacy.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

If I update this Privacy Policy, I will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.

Your Consent

By using Unlimited Sudoku, you consent to this privacy policy and the data handling practices of Apple's iCloud service as they relate to this app.
